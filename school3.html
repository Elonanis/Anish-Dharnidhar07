Modern Business School



Modern Business School (abbv: HBS) is the graduate business school of Modern University. It is located in Boston, Massachusetts. It is one of the top business schools in the world. It offers a wide full-time MBA program, management-related doctoral programs, and many leadership education programs.



It owns Modern Business Publishing. The company publishes business books, leadership articles, case studies, and the monthly Modern Business Review. It is also where the Baker Library/Bloomberg Center is.
